A-Frame Soccer

Navigate around a soccer field built in A-frame with physics provided by Ammojs. Hit the ball with the camera to move it.

Controls:

WMove forward
SMove backward
AStrafe left
DStrafe right
Left click + dragChange camera direction

Available modes:

Free play - Try to get the ball into a goal. No pressure, the only reward is the message "GOAL!" when you succeed.

Scored - Track the number of goals you can score before the ball glitches out of bounds.

This scene is designed for desktop/laptop use only, and is not guaranteed to function on mobile/tablet/vr devices.
